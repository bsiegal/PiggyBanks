Piggy Banks

A counting change money game for kids - by Bess Siegal

How to play

  • Deposit a coin into a piggy bank by dragging and dropping it just above a piggy bank slot.
  • Continue depositing coins into each piggy bank until they are filled with the appropriate amount of money.
  • If you try to put too much in, the coin will be rejected.
  • If you need to break a coin into smaller change, drop it in the 'Need change?' area.
  • If you need to review what you've deposited so far, each piggy bank has a 'tooltip.' (Hover or touch for 2 seconds.)
